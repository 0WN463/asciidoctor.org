[role="unstyled"]
^$$^Superscript^$$^:: One caret (+^+) on either side of a word or phrase makes it superscript.
~$$~Subscript~$$~:: One tilde (+~+) on either side of a word or phrase makes it subscript.

[source]
----
^superscript phrase^

e = mc^2^

~subscript phrase~

H~2~O
----

====
^superscript phrase^

e = mc^2^

~subscript phrase~

H~2~O
====
